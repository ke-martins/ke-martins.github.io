# Personal Webpage
Besides general info about my research and a list of publications, the idea is to communicate the advancements of my Marie Skłodowska-Curie Fellowship lidBathy, which aims at studying sediment exchanges near the shoreline of sandy beaches during storms thanks to the development of innovative depth-inversion approaches based on lidar scanners. This Felloswhip is funded by the European Union’s Horizon 2020 research and innovation program under the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Grant Agreement 887867 (lidBathy).
